# 舔狗进化论 · Design Philosophy

## Movement Name
**舔狗进化论** (Simping Evolution Theory)

## The Philosophy
A brutalist manifesto masquerading as a poster — three evolutionary stages rendered as scientific specimen cards. The humor lives in the taxonomy: what begins as pathetic and solitary becomes a numbers game, then graduates to a philosophy. Color progresses from cold despair (gray) through warm yearning (amber) to aggressive dominance (crimson red), as if documenting an animal species behavioral escalation under controlled observation.

## Visual Expression
Three panels as specimen cards. Chromatic escalation: gray → amber → crimson. Bold red specimen badges. Geometric wolf silhouettes that evolve from hunched/sad to standing/alert to aggressive. Massive slogan: "舔一个叫舔狗 · 舔100个你就是战狼" in white stroke type at bottom.
